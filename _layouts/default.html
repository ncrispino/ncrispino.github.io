Nick Crispino

I am a first-year PhD student at Washington University in St. Louis, where I am part of the McKelvey School of Engineering and work on natural language processing. I am a member of the WashU NLP Group, advised by Professor Chenguang Wang.

GitHub  /  Twitter  /  CV

Research

I'm interested in better understanding how large language models work and trying to improve our control of model outputs in an interpretable way. I am also currently interested in exploring LLM abilities on various reasoning tasks and expanding the capabilities of open-source models.
